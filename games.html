NieR Games

2010

NieR Replicant

When his sister Yonah falls vicim to a deadly disease, main character “Nier” goes on a treacherous journey across a ruined world to find a cure. The more he explores, the more connections he makes — yet, the more he becomes aware of the darker secrets that lie within the world he so treasures living in.

This game was exclusively released for the Playstation 3.







NieR Gestalt

Almost the exact same plot as the original NieR Replicant — except, for Western releases, the developers decided it would be a better idea to make the main character a rugged, older man instead. Thus, in NieR Gestalt, the main character “Nier” is instead made to be father to the sickly Yonah.

This game was exclusively released for the Xbox 360 to a Western audience.

NieR Automata

In the far future of the events of NieR Replicant, androids 2B and 9S are part of a larger movement to defend Earth from being completely taken over by aliens been occupied by aliens, who fight using an army of Machine Lifeforms.

Critically acclaimed, Automata was released worldwide on the following platforms: PlayStation 4, Windows, Xbox One, and Nintendo Switch.
