ACT I

A young Nier starts his journey to save his sister Yonah, meeting new friends and confronting new foes along the way.

General Tips



For causal players:

  • Take your time with quests.
  • Find fighting combos that work for you.
  • Regular breaks help to process the story.

For "platinum" players:

  • Cherish your time.
  • Unlock fast travel ASAP.
  • All ACT I quests have time limits.

For combat:

  • It's okay to not level up your weapons!
  • Get good at dodging and defending.
  • Find a weapon and a magic style that works.

For item collection:

  • Collect as much as you can while playing!
  • Even simple things are important.
  • Make lists and keep track of what you need.
